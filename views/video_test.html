WHY NEURODATA?

The revolution in neuroscientific data acquisition is creating an analysis challenge. We leverage cloud-computing technologies to enable large-scale neurodata storing, exploring, analyzing, and modeling, empowering scientists globally to generate and test theories of brain function and dysfunction.
